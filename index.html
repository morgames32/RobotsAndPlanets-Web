About the Game

Stellar Odyssey is an action-packed 2D platformer that takes you on an interstellar adventure across unique planets. Fight epic bosses, solve puzzles, and collect artifacts to save the galaxy from the encroaching darkness.

Features

  • Vibrant visuals inspired by games like Donkey Kong Tropical Freeze and Rayman Legends.
  • Challenging gameplay with creative levels and mechanics.
  • Epic soundtrack and immersive sound effects.
  • Available on Steam.
