About the Game

Robots and Planets is an action-packed 2D platformer that takes you on an interstellar adventure across unique planets. Fight epic bosses, solve puzzles, and collect artifacts to save the galaxy from darkness.

Dynamic Gameplay

Experience fluid movement and tight controls as you traverse challenging levels. Each planet offers unique mechanics to keep you on your toes.

Epic Boss Fights

Test your skills against massive, screen-filling bosses with unique attack patterns. Victory will require strategy and precision.

Puzzles and Exploration

Solve intricate puzzles and uncover hidden paths. Exploration is rewarded with powerful upgrades and collectibles.
